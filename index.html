Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage site in Agra, India. Built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Eiffel Tower

An iconic iron tower built in 1889, symbol of Paris and one of the most famous landmarks in the world.

Paris, France

Colosseum

Ancient Roman amphitheater and one of the greatest works of Roman architecture and engineering.

Rome, Italy

Great Wall of China

Series of fortifications built to protect China from invasions. Stretches over 13,000 miles.

China
